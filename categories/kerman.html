About Kerman Rugs

Kerman rugs are artistic and refined Persian carpets, celebrated for intricate floral patterns, luxurious wool, and extraordinary craftsmanship.

The History of Kerman Rugs

Kerman has been a major center of Persian carpet production for centuries. During the nineteenth and early twentieth centuries, Kerman rugs gained international recognition for artistic excellence and became highly desirable in European and American markets.

Distinctive Design Features

Kerman rugs are distinguished by elaborate and highly decorative motifs.

  • Floral medallions
  • Vase designs
  • All-over floral fields
  • Curvilinear patterns
  • Elegant arabesques
  • Nature-inspired motifs

Premium Materials and Craftsmanship

Authentic Kerman rugs are woven using premium-quality wool and traditional Persian techniques.

  • Soft, lustrous wool
  • Fine knotting
  • Detailed craftsmanship
  • Durable construction
  • Exceptional finishing

Traditional Color Palette

Kerman rugs are admired for sophisticated colors.

  • Soft red
  • Ivory
  • Navy blue
  • Rose tones
  • Beige
  • Gold
  • Sage green

Why Choose a Kerman Rug?

Kerman rugs offer exceptional artistic detail, fine Persian craftsmanship, luxurious texture, timeless elegance, and strong collector appeal.
