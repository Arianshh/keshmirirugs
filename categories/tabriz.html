About Tabriz Rugs

Tabriz rugs are among the finest and most prestigious Persian carpets ever created, renowned for extraordinary craftsmanship and intricate designs.

The History of Tabriz Rugs

As one of Iran's oldest cultural and commercial centers, Tabriz has played a significant role in the development of Persian carpet weaving. During the Safavid Dynasty, the city became a major artistic hub where master weavers produced some of the most celebrated carpets in Persian history.

Distinctive Design Features

Tabriz rugs are famous for remarkable diversity of designs and superior detail.

  • Central medallions
  • Floral masterpieces
  • Hunting scenes
  • Garden designs
  • Vase patterns
  • Tree of Life motifs
  • Pictorial and artistic compositions

Premium Materials and Craftsmanship

Authentic Tabriz rugs are produced using the finest materials available.
