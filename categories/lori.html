About Lori Rugs

Lori rugs are authentic and expressive tribal carpets, handwoven by Luri tribes in western and southwestern Iran.

The History of Lori Rugs

The Luri people have maintained a strong nomadic heritage for generations. Their weaving traditions developed as an essential part of daily life, with rugs serving practical, decorative, and cultural purposes.

Distinctive Design Features

Lori rugs are characterized by bold and highly personal designs.

  • Geometric medallions
  • Diamond patterns
  • Stylized animals and birds
  • Tribal symbols
  • Tree of Life motifs
  • Simple yet powerful borders

Premium Materials and Craftsmanship

Authentic Lori rugs are hand-knotted using locally sourced wool renowned for strength and resilience.

  • Hand-spun wool
  • Natural vegetable dyes
  • Durable construction
  • Thick and comfortable pile

Traditional Color Palette

Lori rugs are admired for rich and earthy colors.

  • Deep red
  • Indigo blue
  • Ivory
  • Brown
  • Terracotta
  • Soft green accents

Why Choose a Lori Rug?

Lori rugs offer authentic tribal craftsmanship, unique handmade character, exceptional durability, rich cultural heritage, and timeless decorative appeal.
