About the Collection

Lori rugs are authentic and expressive tribal carpets, handwoven by Luri tribes in western and southwestern Iran.

The Luri people have maintained a strong nomadic heritage for generations. Their weaving traditions developed as an essential part of daily life, with rugs serving practical, decorative, and cultural purposes.
