About Baluch Rugs

Distinctive tribal carpets admired for intricate geometric patterns, rich dark colors, and remarkable craftsmanship. Compact tribal rugs with dark dramatic colors and symbolic detail. At Keshmiri Rugs, this collection is presented for clients who appreciate Persian heritage, handmade character and timeless decorative value.

Design Character

  • Repeating tribal patterns
  • Stylized floral elements
  • Symbolic tribal motifs
  • Fine detailing

A beautiful choice for collectors, interior designers and homeowners looking for authentic craftsmanship, visual warmth and cultural identity.
