About Baluch Rugs

Baluch rugs are among the most distinctive tribal carpets of the Persian world, admired for intricate geometric patterns, rich dark colors, and remarkable craftsmanship.

The History of Baluch Rugs

Baluch weaving traditions have been passed down through generations, preserving a unique artistic language shaped by tribal culture and desert landscapes. Historically, Baluch rugs were woven for personal use, trade, and ceremonial purposes, reflecting the daily life and beliefs of their weavers.

Distinctive Design Features

Baluch rugs are characterized by compact designs and symbolic tribal detail.

  • Geometric motifs
  • Prayer rug designs
  • Repeating tribal patterns
  • Stylized floral elements
  • Symbolic tribal motifs
  • Fine detailing

Premium Materials and Craftsmanship

Traditional Baluch rugs are hand-knotted using high-quality wool and natural dyes.

  • Fine wool pile
  • Soft texture
  • Durable construction
  • Traditional hand-spinning methods

Traditional Color Palette

Baluch rugs are known for their darker, dramatic colors.

  • Deep burgundy
  • Dark red
  • Navy blue
  • Brown
  • Black
  • Ivory highlights

Baluch rugs provide authentic tribal artistry, rich historical character, versatile decorative appeal, durable craftsmanship, and excellent collector value. Keshmiri Rugs proudly offers carefully selected Baluch rugs that showcase the enduring beauty of this remarkable weaving tradition.
