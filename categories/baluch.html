About the Collection

Baluch rugs are among the most distinctive tribal carpets of the Persian world, admired for intricate geometric patterns, rich dark colors, and remarkable craftsmanship.

Baluch weaving traditions have been passed down through generations, preserving a unique artistic language shaped by tribal culture and desert landscapes. Historically, Baluch rugs were woven for personal use, trade, and ceremonial purposes, reflecting the daily life and beliefs of their weavers.
