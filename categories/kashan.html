About Kashan Rugs

Kashan rugs are among the most iconic and prestigious Persian carpets, renowned for elegant floral designs, rich color palettes, and exceptional craftsmanship.

The History of Kashan Rugs

Kashan has been a center of artistic excellence for centuries. The city's carpet-weaving tradition flourished during the Safavid Dynasty, when master artisans created some of the most celebrated Persian carpets in history.

Distinctive Design Features

Kashan rugs are famous for sophisticated and symmetrical designs.

  • Central medallions
  • Shah Abbasi flowers
  • Curvilinear floral patterns
  • Elegant arabesques
  • Intricate corner designs
  • Garden-inspired compositions

Premium Materials and Craftsmanship

Authentic Kashan rugs are meticulously hand-knotted using premium materials.

  • Fine wool pile
  • Silk highlights
  • Cotton foundations
  • High knot density
  • Exceptional attention to detail

Traditional Color Palette

Kashan rugs are known for rich and elegant colors.

  • Deep ruby red
  • Navy blue
  • Ivory
  • Gold
  • Beige
  • Soft green accents

Why Choose a Kashan Rug?

Kashan rugs are valued for world-renowned reputation, timeless elegance, exceptional craftsmanship, investment potential, and luxurious decorative appeal.
