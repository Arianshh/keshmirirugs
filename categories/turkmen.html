About Turkmen Rugs

Turkmen rugs are recognizable tribal carpets admired for distinctive geometric motifs, rich red tones, and centuries-old weaving traditions.

The History of Turkmen Rugs

For centuries, Turkmen tribes have woven rugs as an essential part of their nomadic lifestyle. These carpets served as floor coverings, tent decorations, storage bags, and symbols of tribal identity.

Distinctive Design Features

Turkmen rugs are instantly recognizable for repeating geometric motifs known as Guls.

  • Gul motifs
  • Geometric medallions
  • Tribal symbols
  • Repeating patterns
  • Symmetrical layouts
  • Strong decorative borders

Premium Materials and Craftsmanship

Authentic Turkmen rugs are woven using high-quality wool and traditional tribal techniques.

  • Dense hand-knotting
  • Durable wool pile
  • Fine craftsmanship
  • Long-lasting construction

Traditional Color Palette

Turkmen rugs are known for iconic deep colors.

  • Deep red
  • Burgundy
  • Dark brown
  • Ivory
  • Black accents
  • Rust tones

Why Choose a Turkmen Rug?

Turkmen rugs provide authentic tribal heritage, distinctive geometric beauty, exceptional durability, strong collector appeal, and timeless decorative value.
