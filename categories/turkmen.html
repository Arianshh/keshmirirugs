About the Collection

Turkmen rugs are recognizable tribal carpets admired for distinctive geometric motifs, rich red tones, and centuries-old weaving traditions.

For centuries, Turkmen tribes have woven rugs as an essential part of their nomadic lifestyle. These carpets served as floor coverings, tent decorations, storage bags, and symbols of tribal identity.
