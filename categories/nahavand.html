About Nahavand Rugs

Nahavand rugs are Persian village carpets known for durability, vibrant colors, and striking geometric patterns.

The History of Nahavand Rugs

Nahavand has a long tradition of carpet weaving influenced by neighboring Kurdish and Persian communities. Local artisans have preserved their techniques for generations, producing rugs that are both beautiful and durable.

Distinctive Design Features

Nahavand rugs typically feature bold geometric compositions and strong visual structure.

  • Diamond medallions
  • Geometric floral motifs
  • Repeating tribal patterns
  • Angular borders
  • Herati-inspired designs
  • Symmetrical layouts

Premium Materials and Craftsmanship

Authentic Nahavand rugs are hand-knotted using high-quality wool and traditional village weaving techniques.

  • Durable wool pile
  • Dense construction
  • Long-lasting performance
  • Skilled hand craftsmanship

Traditional Color Palette

Classic Persian colors create warmth and elegance.

  • Rich red
  • Navy blue
  • Ivory
  • Rust
  • Brown
  • Soft green highlights

Why Choose a Nahavand Rug?

Nahavand rugs provide excellent durability, authentic village craftsmanship, distinctive geometric designs, outstanding value, and timeless Persian character.
