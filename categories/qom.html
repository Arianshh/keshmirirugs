About Qom Rugs

Qom rugs are among the most luxurious and valuable Persian carpets, renowned for silk construction, breathtaking detail, and exceptional craftsmanship.

The History of Qom Rugs

Although Qom's carpet-weaving tradition is relatively modern compared to other Persian weaving centers, it quickly earned an international reputation for producing some of the finest handmade carpets ever created.

Distinctive Design Features

Qom rugs are celebrated for intricate and elegant designs.

  • Floral medallions
  • Garden scenes
  • Tree of Life designs
  • Hunting scenes
  • Islamic architectural motifs
  • Highly detailed pictorial compositions

Premium Materials and Craftsmanship

Authentic Qom rugs are often woven from the finest silk available.

  • Pure silk construction
  • Silk and wool combinations
  • Extremely high knot density
  • Exceptional detailing
  • Luxurious texture and sheen

Traditional Color Palette

Qom rugs feature elegant and sophisticated colors enhanced by silk brilliance.

  • Ivory
  • Gold
  • Navy blue
  • Burgundy
  • Emerald green
  • Beige
  • Soft pastel accents

Why Choose a Qom Rug?

Qom rugs offer luxury craftsmanship, pure silk artistry, exceptional investment potential, museum-quality designs, and worldwide prestige.
