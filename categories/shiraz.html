About Shiraz Rugs

Shiraz rugs are celebrated tribal carpets of southern Iran, admired for bold geometric designs, rich natural colors, and strong nomadic heritage.

The History of Shiraz Rugs

The region surrounding Shiraz has long been home to numerous tribal groups, including the Qashqai, Khamseh, and Luri peoples. These communities developed unique weaving traditions that continue to influence Shiraz rug production today.

Distinctive Design Features

Shiraz rugs are recognized for expressive and highly individual designs.

  • Geometric medallions
  • Stylized birds and animals
  • Tribal symbols
  • Diamond patterns
  • Tree of Life designs
  • Decorative borders

Premium Materials and Craftsmanship

Authentic Shiraz rugs are woven using high-quality wool and traditional tribal techniques.

  • Hand-spun wool
  • Natural dyes
  • Durable hand-knotting
  • Thick and resilient pile

Traditional Color Palette

Shiraz rugs often display vibrant and natural colors.

  • Deep red
  • Indigo blue
  • Ivory
  • Orange
  • Brown
  • Soft green

Why Choose a Shiraz Rug?

Shiraz rugs offer authentic tribal artistry, rich Persian heritage, unique handmade character, exceptional durability, and strong collector appeal.
