About Hamadan Rugs

Hamadan rugs are diverse and historically significant Persian village carpets, celebrated for durability, bold geometric designs, and exceptional value.

The History of Hamadan Rugs

Hamadan is one of the oldest cities in Iran and has long served as a major center for carpet production. Hundreds of surrounding villages contribute to the region's weaving heritage, each bringing unique patterns and techniques to the Hamadan rug tradition.

Distinctive Design Features

Hamadan rugs are known for strong geometric compositions and practical beauty.

  • Central medallions
  • Geometric floral motifs
  • Diamond patterns
  • Tribal-inspired borders
  • Herati designs
  • Repeating field motifs

Premium Materials and Craftsmanship

Authentic Hamadan rugs are hand-knotted using high-quality wool sourced from local sheep.

  • Durable wool pile
  • Strong cotton foundations
  • Dense weaving techniques
  • Long-lasting construction

Traditional Color Palette

The rich color combinations enhance their visual appeal and versatility.

  • Deep red
  • Navy blue
  • Ivory
  • Brown
  • Rust
  • Soft green accents

Why Choose a Hamadan Rug?

Hamadan rugs offer outstanding durability, authentic Persian village character, excellent value, versatile decorative appeal, and timeless craftsmanship.
