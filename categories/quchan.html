About Quchan Rugs

Quchan rugs are renowned for bold tribal aesthetics, exceptional wool quality, and strong Kurdish influence from northeastern Iran.

The History of Quchan Rugs

The city of Quchan has long been home to Kurdish communities whose weaving traditions shaped the region's distinctive rug style. For centuries, local artisans produced carpets known for durability, originality, and cultural significance.

Distinctive Design Features

Quchan rugs reflect both Kurdish and Persian artistic influences.

  • Geometric medallions
  • Tribal motifs
  • Repeating diamond patterns
  • Stylized floral elements
  • Strong borders
  • Symmetrical compositions

Premium Materials and Craftsmanship

Authentic Quchan rugs are hand-knotted using locally sourced wool known for strength and resilience.

  • High-quality wool
  • Natural dyes
  • Dense weaving
  • Long-lasting durability

Traditional Color Palette

Typical colors create a bold and balanced visual appearance.

  • Rich red
  • Dark blue
  • Ivory
  • Brown
  • Terracotta
  • Soft green accents

Why Choose a Quchan Rug?

Quchan rugs are valued for authentic tribal heritage, distinctive geometric designs, exceptional durability, collector appeal, and timeless Persian craftsmanship.
