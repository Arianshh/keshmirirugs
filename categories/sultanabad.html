About Sultanabad Rugs

Sultanabad rugs are admired for grand scale patterns, elegant village character, and decorative Persian beauty suited to both classic and modern interiors.

The History of Sultanabad Rugs

Sultanabad, historically associated with the Arak region, became known for producing highly decorative Persian carpets with graceful proportions and strong artistic identity. These rugs have long been appreciated for their balance of village charm and refined design.

Distinctive Design Features

Sultanabad rugs often combine spacious layouts with elegant floral and geometric motifs.

  • Large-scale floral patterns
  • Open field compositions
  • Decorative borders
  • Soft geometric rhythm
  • Balanced medallion designs
  • Timeless Persian character

Premium Materials and Craftsmanship

Authentic Sultanabad rugs are hand-knotted with durable wool and traditional weaving techniques.

  • Quality wool pile
  • Cotton foundations
  • Hand-knotted construction
  • Durable everyday performance

Traditional Color Palette

Their colors are warm, decorative, and easy to place in elegant homes.

  • Rust red
  • Ivory
  • Navy blue
  • Soft blue
  • Beige
  • Gold accents

Why Choose a Sultanabad Rug?

Sultanabad rugs offer decorative elegance, strong Persian identity, practical durability, versatile interiors, and lasting visual appeal.
