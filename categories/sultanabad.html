About the Collection

Sultanabad rugs are admired for grand scale patterns, elegant village character, and decorative Persian beauty suited to both classic and modern interiors.

Sultanabad, historically associated with the Arak region, became known for producing highly decorative Persian carpets with graceful proportions and strong artistic identity. These rugs have long been appreciated for their balance of village charm and refined design.
